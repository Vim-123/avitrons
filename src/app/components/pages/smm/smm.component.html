Facebook

Facebook is the most popular online social media platform that provides highly targeted paid ads as well as organic posts, enabling brands to showcase their product or services in front of targeted customers or audiences.

Twitter is a free social media platform where users broadcast posts known as tweets. These tweets consist of text, videos, photos, or links. Twitter helps to build community interest for its brand and generate leads.

Instagram

Instagram empowers brands or companies or individuals to promote their products or services on its platform. Instagram assists brands to connect with a vast audience, increasing brand awareness, and maximising sales.

Linkedin is a social media platform that is used by top professionals and companies. LinkedIn helps businesses to make connections, generate leads, improve brand awareness, and many more.
